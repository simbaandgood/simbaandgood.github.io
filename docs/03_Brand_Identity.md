# Workstream 3: Brand & Identity

## 1. Brand Name Strategy

### Corporate / Venture Studio
*   **Primary:** **Simba & Good** (Emotional, mission-driven).
*   **Alternative:** **Axiom Ventures** (More traditional VC feel, if needed for investor meetings).
*   **Recommendation:** Stick with **Simba & Good** everywhere. It’s unique, memorable, and tells the story instantly.

### Consumer / App
*   **Primary:** **Simba ID** (Clear, functional, ties to parent brand).
*   **Alternative:** **PawPrint** (More generic, but friendly).
*   **Recommendation:** **Simba ID**. It leverages the parent brand’s trust and clearly communicates the value proposition (Identity).

## 2. Brand Voice & Tone
*   **Apple:** Minimal, confident, human-centric. "Technology that feels like magic."
*   **Stripe/Linear:** Professional, precise, developer-friendly. "Infrastructure for the internet."

## 3. Social Media Strategy

### Instagram (@simbaandgood)
*   **Focus:** Visual storytelling, emotional connection.
*   **Content Pillars:** 
    1.  Rescue Stories (Before/After).
    2.  Behind-the-Scenes (Team coding, design process).
    3.  Impact Metrics (Live counters of dogs helped).
*   **Bio:** `Building profitable tech to fund animal welfare. 🐾💻 The future of rescue is self-sustaining.`

### LinkedIn (Simba & Good)
*   **Focus:** Investor relations, talent acquisition, B2B partnerships.
*   **Content Pillars:** 
    1.  Venture Studio Insights (How we build SaaS).
    2.  Impact Reporting (Transparent financials).
    3.  Industry Thought Leadership (Future of pet tech/AI).
*   **Bio:** `A venture studio building high-margin SaaS to create a permanent funding engine for global animal welfare.`

### X / Twitter (@simbaandgood)
*   **Focus:** Real-time updates, community engagement, tech culture.
*   **Content Pillars:** 
    1.  Build-in-public updates.
    2.  Tech/AI news commentary.
    3.  Direct engagement with pet owners and founders.
*   **Bio:** `YC-style studio for animal welfare. Building the economic engine for rescue. 🚀🐕`
