# Workstream 1: Strategic Core

## 1. Venture Studio Operating Model (YC-Style)

### The Incubation Cycle
We do not just invest; we build. Each venture follows a rigorous 4-stage lifecycle:

1.  **Ideation (Weeks 1-4):** Internal brainstorming focused on high-margin SaaS opportunities in the pet/animal tech space. Validated against market size and impact potential.
2.  **MVP Build (Months 2-4):** A dedicated "Pod" (1 PM, 2 Eng, 1 Design) builds the Minimum Viable Product using studio capital. No external fundraising yet.
3.  **Validation (Months 5-8):** Launch to early adopters. Target: $10k MRR or 5 pilot enterprise clients.
4.  **Spin-Out (Month 9+):** Once Product-Market Fit (PMF) is achieved, we raise a Seed round from external VCs using **SAFE agreements**. The studio retains a **20-30% equity stake** and transitions leadership to an external CEO.

### Equity & Profit Allocation
*   **Studio Stake:** 20-30% in each portfolio company.
*   **Profit Flow:** As SaaS companies scale, a percentage of net profits is allocated to the **Animal Welfare Foundation**.
*   **Exit Strategy:** Long-term hold for recurring revenue. Strategic exits (acquisitions) provide lump-sum capital for large infrastructure projects (e.g., building a sanctuary).

## 2. Grant Funding Strategy ($1M Target)

### Narrative
"We are building the economic engine for animal welfare. We don't need your money to survive; we need it to scale our impact faster."

### Target Donors
1.  **Impact-First Foundations:** Open Society Foundations, Gates Foundation (Global Health/Dev), Mozilla (Open Tech).
2.  **Corporate CSR:** Chewy, Rover, Petco, Purina. Focus on their "Innovation" or "Tech for Good" grants.
3.  **Government Grants:** USDA (Agricultural tech), NSF (AI/ML research).

### Use of Funds
*   **40% Talent:** Hiring senior engineers and product leads to build the first two SaaS MVPs.
*   **30% Infrastructure:** Cloud costs (AWS/GCP), AI model training, legal entity setup.
*   **30% Impact Seed:** Immediate funding for the first rescue operations to prove the "Impact" side of the model and generate early storytelling content.
